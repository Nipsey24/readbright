💡 Helpful Reading Tips

🖊️

Use a Finger

Point to each word as you read. It helps your brain focus on one word at a time.

🕐

Take Your Time

Slow reading is smart reading. There is no race. Every reader goes at their own pace.

🎧

Listen First

Press the Read Aloud button to hear a word or sentence before you try to read it yourself.

🌈

Colour the Words

Use coloured rulers or overlays on paper. Try yellow — many dyslexic readers love it!

💪

Celebrate Mistakes

Mistakes mean you are trying. Every mistake teaches your brain something new. Go you!

😴

Rest Your Eyes

After reading for 20 minutes, look at something far away for 20 seconds. Your eyes will thank you!
