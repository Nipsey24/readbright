📖 About Dyslexia

Understanding is the first step to helping.

🧠

What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a learning difference that affects how the brain processes written and spoken language. It mainly impacts reading, spelling, and writing — but it has nothing to do with intelligence.

Many brilliant thinkers, artists, and scientists are dyslexic. It is estimated that 1 in 10 people worldwide has dyslexia.

🔍

Common Signs

  • Difficulty recognising letters and words
  • Spelling the same word differently each time
  • Slower reading compared to peers
  • Mixing up similar letters like b, d, p, q
  • Trouble remembering sequences (e.g. alphabet, months)
  • Difficulty following multi-step instructions
💡

How We Can Help

  • 🔊 Text-to-Speech — hearing words helps with recognition
  • 🔤 Dyslexia Fonts — specially designed to reduce confusion
  • 🌈 Colour overlays — reduce visual stress on the page
  • 📏 Reading rulers — keep your place on the line
  • 🎮 Games — make learning engaging and fun
